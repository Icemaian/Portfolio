My capstone project was to work on a team who had a diverse range of talents and several IBM software engineers to develope a Chatbot who's focus was to help incoming students at Liberty with questions about campus. As one of the main developers I was in charge of the Chatbot's conversation design, implementation, and conversation training. Using IBM's Watson assistant and the integrated machine learning techniques to recognize sentence structure and context so the end user was able converse with the Chatbot and retrieve the desired information.
